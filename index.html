FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Asset Community?

Created in 2023, to provide businesses and entrepreneurs with exclusive access to a private ecosystem, Asset Community now has over 500+ members nationwide. Over the last year we have gathered thousands of views and followers, developing a polished ecosystem focused on creating and multiplying, high level access and opportunity to all members. However, due to the high demand, the community began limiting access to invitations only.

In 2024, Sahil Gawade, redesigned Asset Community and spend a year building the full digital platform, academy, networks and partners to bring forth one of the most exclusive entrepreneurial ecosystem and desired memberships in the nation. we are very proud to welcome you.

How can you be the member of Asset Community?

Join our waitlist at 'Checkout Asset' to find out when we re-open.

What is the price of the membership?

All existing members will be grandfathered into the same membership fees. When Asset Community reopens in 2025, the price will increase.

How does the 5 days refund garantee work?

You have an option to cancel your membership within 5-day period starting from the day you made the purchase to get a full refund.

Is the membership available internationally?

Yes, we currently working to have waitlist for people from over different countries. Soon the availability for Asset Community will be worldwide.

Have any other questions?

Our official support email address is asset.coo@gmail.com. Whether you have questions about our services, need assitance with your account, or want to provide feedback, our team is here to help. Please allow 2 business days to hear back from us.
